What is Chronological Age?

Chronological age is the time that has passed since a person’s birth to the present day. It is a straightforward measure that can be important in various settings like education, healthcare, or legal matters. Unlike biological age, which refers to how old your body seems based on health and lifestyle factors, chronological age is simply the number of years, months, and days you've been alive.
